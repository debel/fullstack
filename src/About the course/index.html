in percents of the maximum final grade
  • Attendence and Participation - 5%

  • Project - 55%

General Project Requirments (55%)

  1. Teams must develop a full stack micro service
  2. Teams must choose and follow an agile process ({{Agile Processes}})
  3. Teams must choose and follow a development process ({{Development Processes}})
  4. Teams must describe their processes and have a detailed history of members' activities (~1 page of text)
  5. Teams must use uniform development environments and write and use automation scripts
  6. Teams must write front-end code, back-end code, unit tests and end-to-end tests
  7. Teams must set up monitorting into the state of their systems
  8. Teams must document thier code, apis, and (code) design decisions
  9. Teams will have a weekly report session with the professor /online/

Project Ideas:

  • To-do service
  • Avatar service
  • When-This-Then-That service
  • Aggregation service
  • Chat service
  • Game (e.g. Chess) service
  • Social media service
  • Flights search service
  • AUBG related service

Quiz (25%)

The quiz will contain around 25 questions and should take maximum 40 minutes to complete
  • multiple choice
  • fill in the blanks
  • find a bug in - / analyze a code snippet
  • explain a concept / process

Case study report (15%)

Write a 3 page report analyzing the technical aspects of a popular web site or your own previous project. The report should:
  • Describe the architecture of the application
  • Describe the application's components
  • List techologies and processes used in the project
  • Describe the communication patterns of the application
  • List 3 positive things about the app
  • List 3 negative things about the app

Attendence and Participation - 5%

There are 10 sessions of this class. Attendance brings 0.5% for each.
Discussion, knowledge sharing mentoring and pear coding are all expected.
